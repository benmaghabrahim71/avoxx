General Rules

1
No deathmatching
Killing without proper roleplay reason is forbidden. Always RP before engaging.
2
No metagaming
Using out-of-character information (e.g. Discord, names above heads) inside the RP is not allowed.
3
No powergaming
Forcing actions on other players or performing unrealistic actions in roleplay is prohibited.
4
Stay in character
Use /b for OOC chat in IC chat, and only when necessary.
5
Respect other players
Harassment, racism, sexism, or any kind of hate speech leads to immediate ban.

Combat & RP Rules

6
No revenge killing (RK)
After death you forget the event. Do not return to the same spot for 15 minutes.
7
Fear roleplay
Show appropriate fear when held at gunpoint or outnumbered. Heroes die fast.
8
No car ramming
Ramming on foot players outside of legitimate RP scenarios is forbidden.
9
Robbery limits
Max $20,000 cash or 1 weapon per robbery. No robbing in safe zones.
10
No combat logging
Quitting during a roleplay or combat scenario will result in a ban.

Account & Cheating

11
One account per person
Multi-accounting is prohibited unless explicitly approved by management.
12
No cheats / mods
Any third-party tool that gives an unfair advantage is bannable on first offense.
13
No bug abuse
Found a bug? Report it via /report. Abusing it = permanent ban.
14
No real-money trading
Selling/trading accounts, items, or in-game cash for real money outside the shop is forbidden.

Names & Chat

15
Realistic names
Only realistic Firstname_Lastname names. No celebrities, joke names, or special chars.
16
English in /b and global
OOC chat must be in English so admins can understand.
17
No spam
Repeating messages, ads, or capslock = mute and warning.
